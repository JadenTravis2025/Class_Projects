Voyager 1 mission and main focus

Voyager one was a space craft like no other. In 1977 Nasa had luanched its voyager 1 satillite to explore the the planets inside of our solar system. Voyager 1's its main focus was Jupiter and Saturn it would orbit these planets gathering data about them and also taking data from there moons. The spacecraft was tasked with taking high quality images of the moons and planets. This data was crucial for the program and to us here on earth. Voyager 1 had a heavy task of going where noone or no thing had gone before allowing us to land eyes on these objects for the first time in a new way. The studies of these planets give us insight on our own planet and how it came to be formed.
