Overview

The artemis program is bringing us back to the moon. But this time we will be here to stay and establish a lunar presence. The program will set up by three stages. The first will not have a crew aboard this is just a test flight of the 2 crafts orion and the SLS. now for the second it will be manned and this will be a true test of the spacecrafts. Its goal is to orbit around the moon and return back to there home planet. The third stage of this program is the true first step of the Artemis program. This will be histroric, it has been 50 years since humans have set foot on the lunar surface.

Mission Tasks

  • Launch the Orion spacecraft aboard the Space Launch System
  • Conduct crewed lunar flybys and landings
  • Set up lunar gateway
  • Contruction of lunar base

Scientific Objectives

The Scientific obejective for the program once landed on the moon will be a tasks of multiple lunar studies. The moon hides secrets with the consatant bombardment of comets and meteorites on its back side. This means ice could be buried deep inside the crust. The LCROSS has comfirmed this. This will benefit humanity in ways we cannot imagine. For instance water is what is needed for life on the moon this will help us when the base is established for long stays on the lunar surface. Billions of kilograms of water is waiting to be found and studied.

Technology
